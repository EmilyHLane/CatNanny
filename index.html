Meet the Cat Nanny

There have been cats in my family for the majority of my life, always adopted from a shelter, and usually two at a time. A few years ago, I decided to simplify my life and started my small business of being the Cat Nanny.

My pets are Ashe and Shadow, and when I go on vacation, I need to know that they are taken care of and comfortable with their sitter who gives them special attention. This is what I will provide for your pets. You will be able to go on your work travel or vacation knowing that I am there taking care of your pets, following your routine and trying to make their lives as happy and comfortable as if you were there yourself.

I get great satisfaction from what I'm doing right now. Cats are such special animals with their own distinct personalities and truly fill my life with joy.

SPCA Volunteer Cat Socializer

I volunteered as a cat socializer in Shelter Medicine at the SF SPCA for 4 years. I have had training in working with shy/undersocialized kittens and cats, affection eaters, and cats with aggressive behaviour or who get overstimulated easily. I have a very soft voice and gentle approach and am usually very successful in coaxing out even the shyest cat.
